# claude-tips-for-creatives

## what this is

a tips repo for non-technical creatives using Claude.ai and Cursor. think: marketers, designers, UX/UI, PMs. not software engineers.

maintained by mewtwo (@withmewtwo), founder of junohauz.

## voice and style

- lowercase throughout (except proper nouns and product names)
- short sentences. punchy. no em dashes
- no AI-sounding language ("it's worth noting", "dive into", "unleash")
- first-person where relevant
- practical over theoretical — every tip should have a concrete example or use case

## product names to get right

- Lovable ❤️ (always with the heart emoji)
- SheBuilds (one word, capital S and B)
- JunoMoment (our vibe-coding product)
- Claude.ai / Cursor (capitalize correctly)

## contributing

if you want to add a tip:
- keep it under 200 words
- make it specific and actionable
- written in the voice above
- add it to the right section (Claude.ai / Cursor / Creative Workflow)
- update the Table of Contents

open a PR or DM @withmewtwo on X.
